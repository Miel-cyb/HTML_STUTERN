My Favourite Meal

Name of food: JOLLOF

About:

Jollof is an African dish prepared by making stew and adding the rice to it. It can be eaten with any kind of meat or an egg. It in a widely known food,brownish is color and has a salivating aroma. Jollof takes a longer time in its preparation hence on a daily basis people opt for rice with stew.

Ingredients Quantity
Rice One cup
Tomato paste and sauce One large tin
Onion 2 bulbs
Meat One pound
Oil Half bottle

Procedure:

  • Fry the onions, add your blended ginger,garlic and pepper
  • Add the tomato paste and sauce,stir and leave it to simmer
  • Add any prefered spices and salt
  • Add cooked meat to the stew
  • Wash rice and add to the stew,leave it until it's uniformly cooked
